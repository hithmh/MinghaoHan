Short Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Control Science and Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), supervised by Prof. Lixian Zhang. I was a visiting researcher at Delft University of Technology, Department of Cognitive Robotics from Jan 2020 to Jun 2020, cooperating with Prof. Wei Pan. My research interests include control theory, reinforcement learning and their application in intelligent systems such as unmanned ground/aerial vehicles and robotics.

Prior to PhD, I received the B.Eng. degree in Automation from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), Harbin, China, in 2017. During the undergraduate study, I was exchanged to National University of Singapore in 2016, sponsored by China Scholarship Council.

Fields of Interests

Control theory, machine learning, artificial intelligence, robotics.
