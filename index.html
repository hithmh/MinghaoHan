Short Biography

I am currently a second year Ph.D. student in Control Science and Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT). My advisor is Prof. Lixian Zhang in Department of Control Science and Engineering at HIT. My research interests include control theory, reinforcement learning and their application in intelligent systems such as unmaned ground/aerial vechicles and robotics.

Prior to PhD, I received the B.Eng. degree in Automation from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), Harbin, China, in 2017. During the undergraduate study, I was exchanged to National University of Singapore in 2016, sponsored by China Scholarship Council.

Fields of Interests

Switched control, robotics, artificial intellgence, machine learning, decision making and motion planning.
